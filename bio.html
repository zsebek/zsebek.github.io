My Biography

Early Childhood

To start things off, I was born on May 11th, 1998, to my parents Jeffrey and Marsha Sebek. However, I was not alone.

A minute after my birth, my twin brother Jason Sebek was born. We were born in Chicago, and have lived on Chicago's

south side since. My brother Jason and I attended preschool at the Beverly Montessori school, and kindergarten at our neighborhood school, Clissold.

From Clissold, I transferred to Keller Regional Gifted Center, a selective

enrollment school in the Chicago Public School system.

Middle School to College

After 6th grade, I transferred to Whitney M Young Magnet High School, entering in 7th grade at the school's Academic Center Program.

I continued my studies here through high school. During this time I joined the school's Cross Country Team, Tennis Team, and other organizations.

I also began learning Japanese in high school, which led to study abroad experience in Kyoto, Tokyo, and Hiroshima.

Along with other school activities, I am also an Eagle Scout, like my three other brothers.

After attending Whitney Young, I enrolled in Tufts University in the Fall of 2016.

My major is undecided but I have become interested in Economics, Psychology, International Relations, and Computer Science.

At Tufts I have become involved with TedxTufts, Tufts' Japanese Culture Club, The Tufts Daily, Turbo, and WMFO.
